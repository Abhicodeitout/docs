# Terms of service & Privacy policy links

You can provide a Privacy policy and Terms of service link which will render the following text on the sign up page:
- Provided both links: "By signing up, you agree to our [Terms of Service](./toc-privacypolicy) and [Privacy Policy](./toc-privacypolicy)"
- Provided only Terms of service link: "By signing up, you agree to our [Terms of Service](./toc-privacypolicy)"
- Provided only Privacy policy link: "By signing up, you agree to our [Privacy Policy](./toc-privacypolicy)"

SuperTokens.init({

    ]
